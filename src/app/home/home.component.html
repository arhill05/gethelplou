Get Help Lou

Get Help Lou is a free resource for anyone to use to find drug and addiction treatment and rehabilitation centers in or around Louisville, KY. This resource is heavily inspired by the tool Get Help Lex.

What kind of facilities are you interested in?

What this is

This is a tool for anyone who is seeking facilities and centers that assist with addiction and substance abuse.

What this isn't

This tool is not a diagnosis tool, an assessment tool, or a substitute for any kind of treatment. If you or someone else is experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately.
